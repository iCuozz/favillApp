Meet Favilla Blaze: by day a school assistant, by night a supermom hero.

Favilla Blaze is an illustrated, interactive comic for adults and kids who love bright art and stories that don't take themselves too seriously. Step into Favilla's everyday life — between school chaos, snack-time disasters, supermarket missions and the occasional kitchen ninja — and watch her turn the messiest moments into superhero feats.
